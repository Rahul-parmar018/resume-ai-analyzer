# Candidex AI - User Guide

Welcome to Candidex AI! Follow this step-by-step guide to optimize your resume, analyze your career fit, and accelerate your job search.

## 🧭 Step-by-Step User Journey

### 1. Upload & Optimize
1. Navigate to the **Resume Scanner** dashboard.
2. Select your target **Role** (e.g. AI Engineer, Full Stack Developer, Backend Developer).
3. Drag and drop or upload your PDF/TXT resume.
4. Add a specific **Job Description** to compare alignment.
5. Click **Scan Resume** to generate your initial analysis report.

### 2. Reading Your Results Dashboard
- **ATS Score:** View your general match percentage, with detailed section-by-section scoring breakdowns.
- **Resume Health:** Inspect structural completeness metrics, contact checks, action verb count, and metric density indicators.
- **Skill Gaps:** Review missing competencies categorized by importance (Critical, High, Medium, Low).
- **Career Matches:** View your top 5 alternative career paths based on semantic similarity.
- **Market Readiness:** See how well your skills align with current regional market demands and emerging technology trends.

### 3. Career Agent Chat
- Click on the **Career Agent** tab.
- Ask questions like:
  - *"How can I improve my AWS score?"*
  - *"What are the most popular skills in Germany for AI Engineers?"*
  - *"Tell me the formatting guidelines for ATS."*
- The agent will scan your profile, search the knowledge base, and return actionable bullet points.

### 4. Interactive Learning Roadmap
- Follow suggested portfolio projects, recommended certifications, and estimated learning timelines to bridge identified skill gaps.
- Take note of localized interview preparation questions and system design topics.
